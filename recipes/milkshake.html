Milkshake

Description

A cool, refreshing mint milkshake that will have you craving for more. All you need is milk, vanilla ice cream, chocolate syrup, and a few drops of peppermint extract.

Ingredients

  • 4 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup chocolate syrup
  • 1 drop peppermint extract

Steps

  1. Creating the Drink: Combine ice cream, milk, chocolate syrup, and peppermint extract in a blender; blend until smooth.
  2. Serving the Drink: Pour into 2 glasses and serve.
